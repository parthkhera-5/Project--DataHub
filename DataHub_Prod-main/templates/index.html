Features

Here are some of the ways DataHub can help you manage your data better.

STUDENTS DATA :
Manage comprehensive student profiles including personal details, academic history, attendance, and performance records. The system allows quick access and real-time updates, ensuring accuracy and ease of tracking student progress. Streamlined input helps administrators efficiently monitor student data from admission through graduation

FACULTY DATA:
 Easily manage faculty profiles, including their subjects, schedules, qualifications, and availability. The system supports updates in real-time, ensuring accurate timetables and performance tracking. Faculty members can also access their data securely, making it easier to stay organized, update their teaching responsibilities, and communicate with departments and students.

DEPARTMENTS DATA:
Organize and maintain department-specific data such as department heads, courses offered, faculty assignments, and resource allocations. Administrators can easily update department details, monitor staffing, and analyze academic contributions. The centralized system promotes smoother coordination between departments and ensures that all departmental data is current and accessible.

COURSES DATA
: Manage all course-related information, including course codes, titles, descriptions, credit hours, and prerequisites. Faculty assignments and student enrollments are integrated for easy tracking. The system supports real-time updates, ensuring that changes in curriculum or faculty assignments reflect instantly, helping maintain accuracy and improve academic planning.

COLLEGE DATA :
Manage all the data about the colleges that are affiliated to the university and track details like name , address , college id , contact number etc.

EASY DATA MANIPULATION :
 Our web app offers an interactive, user-friendly, and responsive experience with a seamless interface to update your details .
